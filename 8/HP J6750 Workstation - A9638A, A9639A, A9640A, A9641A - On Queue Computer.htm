The HP Workstation J6750 delivers industry-leading performance and unparalleled memory and computer density. It ensures that designs and simulations are completed faster than with any other UNIX two-way workstation. whether used as a deskside configuration optimized for an office environment or a racked solution, the HP J6750's performance and reliability will satisfy the most demanding user. When your customers need ultimate performance for today's toughest designs and simulations with the capacity to handle tomorrow's even bigger workloads, the HP Workstation J6750 is up to the challenge.


  • Dual 875 MHz RISC processors provide higher application performance at a lower price
  • 1 GB SDRAM standard, expandable to 16 GB
  • 2.25 MB on-chip cache enhances system performance
  • Supports one or two 36, 73, and 146 GB 10k rpm and 36 GB 15k rpm Ultra2 SCSI LVD hard disk drives
  • Supports HP-UX 11i technical computing operating environment (TCOE), 11i minimal technical operating environment (MTOE) , and 11.0
